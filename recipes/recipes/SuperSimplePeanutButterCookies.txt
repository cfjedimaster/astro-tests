Ingredients: 
	1 cup of peanut butter
	1/2 cup sugar
	1 egg
Instructions: 
	Add peanut butter, sugar, and egg to a bowl.
	Mix it well.
	Roll into bowls onto parchment paper covered cookie sheet.
	Using a fork, press into each ball.
	Cook in over at 350 degrees for fifteen  minutes.
