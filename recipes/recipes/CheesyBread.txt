Ingredients: 
	2 1/4 teaspoon yeast
	3 1/4 cups flour
	1 teaspoon salt
	1 1/2 cup warm water
	2 cups cheese
Instructions: 
	Add flour, yeast, salt, and water to a bowl.
	Mix it together.
	Possibly add garlic and onion powder.
	Add the cheese to the bowl and mix it together with your hands.
	Take mass out of bowl, pour in a tablespoon of oil, then put the mass back in.
	Cover and leave in a warm spot for 1.5 hours.
	Warm up dutch oven, in the oven (450)
	Put dough and stuff on parchment paper and form it up nicely.
	Put it in the dutch oven. (Maybe sprinkle some cheese on top.)
	Cook for 25 minutes in the oven at 450 degrees.
	5 or so minutes before the end, remove the top to let it crisp.
